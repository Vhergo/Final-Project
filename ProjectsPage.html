2048: Block Bash

A Refreshing Reimagining of the Classic 2048 Mobile Hit

    This project that revitlizes one of the most iconic mobile games of the last decade. The newly implemented mechanics and visuals breath new life into the game.

    New Mechanics
    • Cycling: A feature that allows you to control individual rows and columns
    • Obstacle Blocks: Special blocks that can only be moved by cycling
    • Block Breaking: A feature that allows you to break obstacle blocks for bonus points

    My Roles:
    • Project Manager
    • Programmer: C#
    • Artist: Pixel and Vector Art
    Used Tools:
    • Unity
    • Aseprite
    • Photoshop

Holey Moley

My Submission for the 2023 Global Game Jam

    Taking the theme this game jam was "Roots". My team and I decided to make give the roots a direct impact in the game as the primary antagonist. The core gameplay revolves around a poor mole digging for dear life as the roots burrow into the ground in pursuit.

    Mechanics
    • Mouse-Follow Movement
    • Dash: Gives the player a burst of speed
    • Obstacles: The player must avoid the numerous boulders found underground
    • Grub: Eating these bugs on your way down will give you the strength to break obstacles

    My Roles:
    • Programmer: C#
    Used Tools:
    • Unity

Dice In The Wall

My Submission for the 2022 Game Maker's Tool Kit Game Jam

    This jam's theme was "Role of the Dice". My team decided to make the dice itself the player character. In the name of avoiding overscoping, we decided to go with the tried an true infinite runner. The player needs to adjust both its position and dice orientation to pass the obstacles.

    Mechanics
    • Shift between the three lanes using Q & E
    • Change the dice orientation using the W A S D keys
    • Match dice face that is facing you to the upcoming obstacles

    My Roles:
    • Programmer: C#
    • Artist: 3D and Vector Art
    Used Tools:
    • Unity
    • Blender 3D
